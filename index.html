I am a first-year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Yale University. My research interest is to build algorithms and intelligent systems that are meaningfully useful. Prior to Yale, I was a master student at Princeton working on spatiotemporal modeling, advised by Prof.Olga Russakovsky.

Prior to Yale/Princeton, I graduated from Northwestern University where I worked with Prof. Fabian Bustamante on distributed systems. My senior year project is on Peer-to-Peer Forward Proxy Network. I also worked at Facebook for 2 years as a machine learning engineer, building NLP and feed ranking models for Job Search team.
